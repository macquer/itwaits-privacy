Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 6, 2026

It Waits: A Horror Card Game ("the App") is a single-player mobile game developed by KorkuKart ("we", "us"). This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not collect names, emails, or any personal identifiers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not make network requests except for loading fonts via Google Fonts

Local Data

Game progress (save files, settings, language preference) is stored locally on your device using standard app storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Google Fonts

The App uses the Google Fonts service to load typefaces. Google's privacy policy applies to this service: policies.google.com/privacy. No personal data is sent by the App to Google beyond standard HTTP requests for font files.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. It contains horror-themed content and is intended for a teen and adult audience.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will post the new version at this URL with a revised effective date.
